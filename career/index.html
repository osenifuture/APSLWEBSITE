Introduction

Agoa Power Smile Limited (APSL) specializes in engineering industry and had an exciting opportunity for an enthusiastic Power,Tech, and ICT engineers to join our dynamic team.This permanent position is well suited to an individual that is looking to advance thier career in engineering and gain hands-on experience in a thriving and supportive workplace.

Objectives

Based within the engineering department you will work closely with all areas of engineering to assist with the design,repair,install and maintain client equipments and helping the team to achive agreed targets.This exciting position offers opportunity to progress into higher roles.

  • Making plans using detailed drawing.
  • Preparing estimate and budgets.
